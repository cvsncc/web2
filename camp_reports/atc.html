All India Annual Training Camp, Ezhimala

Experiences

I, CDT Aastha Verma and CDT Tannu got the golden opportunity to visit the prestigious Indian Naval Academy - the initial officer training establishment of Indian Navy and Indian coast guard located in Ezhimala, Kannur district of Kerala. The cadets participated in All India Annual Training Camp for SW Naval Cadets in Ezhimala from 9th January to 20 January 2020. The cadets took part in various competitions like boat pulling , best cadet , semaphore , drill , tug of war and cultural events. Delhi directorate got first position in boat pulling and best cadet competition. The cadets also got to see the renowned academy where they saw Ramanujan auditorium, Aryabhatta academic block , ship stimulator , Achievers club, lighthouse, Marakkar watermanship training center, Arjana baffle firing range, beach, horse riding complex, parade ground, sports ground, rifle club , and Katari mess . The cadets also visited Payyambalam beach, St. Angelo fort , and Muthappan temple in Kannur, the magnificent city of Kerala. The cadets also got classes on personality development , basic life support, and fire fighting . The cadets interacted with various army and navy officers too. Last day was the closing ceremony where all directorates performed in front of the Vice Admiral and many navy officers . The cadets were given a friendly environment where they made friends from all over the country who became like family in the camp. Those unforgettable memories will remain etched in the hearts forever , from living in tents to making chapattis and snake pits , the cadets would miss it all.
