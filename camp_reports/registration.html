View Rules & Regulations

Rules for respective competitions are as follows:

• Drill Competition: Total numbers of cadets: 10(9+1).
Only one team from each institution can participate for SW & SD each.
• Guard Competition: Total numbers of cadets: 08(2+6).
Only one team from each college can participate for SW & SD each.
• Best Cadet: Cadet will be judged on the basis Command, Drill, Turnout, Discipline and General knowledge.
Only one cadet per institution is allowed for SD & SW each.
• Bilingual Debate: Topic: “Terrorism and militancy in the country can be tackled effectively while observing Human Rights.”
Cadets per college: 2 Time Duration: 3 Minute
• Quiz: Theme: Defense Forces of India and NCC.
Cadets per College: 02
• Cultural Competition (Group Song & Group Dance): Only one team per institution is allowed.
Time Limit: 03 minutes (strictly) Maximum number of Cadets: 06
For group dance: Participants need to submit their song at the time of registration, and also send it at cvsnccofficial@gmail.com with college name.
• Tug of war: Only those cadets who took part in Drill and Quarter Guard Competition will be eligible to participate. Cadets should be in uniform during the competition.
NOTE: The organizers reserve the right to vary, alter, modify, or repeal any of the above rules without any prior notification, if so required as they may deem appropriate. The interpretation of these rules by the organizer shall be conclusive. The decision by the organizer for the above stated rules shall be final and binding
