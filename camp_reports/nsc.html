Nau Sainik Camp

Experiences

The All India Nau sainik camp 2019 is and will be a cherishing experience of my life .The camp comprises of nearly a dozen of events, namely â€“ Boat Pulling, Service Subject Examination, Seamanship, Firing, Semaphore, Whaler Rigging, Drill, Best Cadet, Health and Hygiene, Ship Modelling and Line Area. Educational and recreational tours to INS Kursura, TU-142 Aircraft Museum and R.K. Competing with different directorates provided me a vision of the diversity the country has. AINSC being the most prestigious camp that a naval cadet can be part of, has taught me several lessons which will tarry through out my life. Training during the calefactory weather made it the toughest phase to pass through. With various events to cover it provided with immense knowledge . It all defined our mental and physical limits.
On the D- day with positive spirit we began the competitions . With competitions going side along, the days spent there will be always adored. This year, the Delhi Directorate secured 8th position, making a record of securing a single digit rank, almost after a decade. A team of 2 SWs and 3 SDs from each directorate also got an once in a lifetime opportunity of sailing on INS Sumedha and INS Sukanya for a day.
Visiting the Eastern Naval Command i.e. INS CIRCARS and INS VISHWAKARMA was a dream come true for us . In the span of 4 months , i got to develop rugged habits which i will take forward for the coming phases of the life. This camp has surely taught me a lot and has found a safe column in my heart. Altogether, the camp was an electrifying experience. With the competitions like Boat Pulling, Whaler Rigging and firing being held at INS Circars (Eastern Naval Command) and Drill Competition at INS Vishwakarma truly provided us a beatitude experience to all of us, which will definitely be cherished by all of us for life!

Cadet Punarvasu Sharma
