Advance leadership Camp

Experiences

Adcanced Leadership Camp that took place in the beautiful city of coimbatore from 6 dec to 17 dec 2019 was conducted by 2 Tamil Nadu Battery NCC. It was commanded by Lt. Col Girish Parthan. I was a cadet from Delhi Dte. who went to the camp 2500 km away with a motive to learn new things ,develop my personality and polish the qualities in me. And all my expectations from camp met by the end of the camp. It was a wonderful experience that started with a energetic inaugural speech by the Commandant sir. From the next day onwards we had different classes on Personality Development that were taken by highly qualified professionals and also by Army officers . The main and most informative part were the clasees taken by C- labs. Cadets were given a brief overview of all the tests that a cadet has to undergo during the 5 days of SSB. Col. Sabu who was an ex-Psychologist in SSB Allahabad did most to help the students , took OIR test of the cadets , took PPDT and analyse the performance on individual level of all cadets. He also conducted Public Speaking competition, Group Discussion and Debate among the cadets. Different such classes on different topics took place till 13th Dec. On 14th nd 15th Dec, a team of ex-accessors of SSB came and gave 2 day capsule that include basics to crack SSB. By this all the learning part of camp came to an end. Second last dat was the day to visit some famous place. Cadets were taken to the famous Isha Foundation and we all enjoyed the landscape their and prayed to AADIYOGI SHIVA. The last evening was a great one with Cultural Night where the Commandant himself danced with cadets on the stage and ended the camp with a delightful Dinner. On 17th we cadets from 17 dte.s headed back to our place with a more learned and polished version of ourself. Other experiences like Ragda and making a kot of friends from different parts of the country were just like we all do in other camps

Cadet Pushpak Dhami
