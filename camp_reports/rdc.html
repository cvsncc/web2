Republic Day Camp 2020

NCC Republic Day Camp is the culmination of all NCC Training activities. RDC is held at Garrison Parade Ground, Delhi Cantt from 01 to 29 Jan. 2250 Selected NCC Cadets from 17 directorates attend the Camp. The Camp is inaugurated by the Vice President of India and culminates with Prime Minister's Rally on 28 Jan. During the camp visit of Raksha Mantri, Cabinet Ministers, Chief Minister of Delhi, three Service Chiefs and various State Ministers/VIPs are also organised. During the RDC, various competitions are conducted amongst the 17 NCC Directorates to decide the Champion Directorate for award of Prime Minister's Banner.

Experiences

The Republic Day Camp 2020 was special not just because i was a part of it. It was special because i witnessed glorifying portrayal of the values of NCC and it's immense diversity. Bringing unique ambience and culture from various streets of the country , RDC in itself is a auspicious jamboree.

RDC has truly become one of the greatest phases of my life. Judged as the greatest camp for a cadet , i have got so many reasons to tell why. The PT during Chilled Winter Morning to the guard duty after midnight, All that will be cherished for a long time.

It has been a great experience for each of us. Showing Creativity in Line Area Competition and maintenance of the Barracks , We got to learn new skills and crafts. Doing The Drill in early hours , going to group discussion in the late afternoon , organizing the refreshment break for the cadets and the geru chuna till midnight . I can proudly say We all have successfully defined new limits for our bodies physically and mentally. While strictly involved in our ever tiring work , We were still singing 90's songs in our rooms , enjoying cultural performances in the auditorium and Making great bonds with our fellow cadets and also cadets of other directorates. Meanwhile all this i learnt to to take short naps in the mess and even during fall in . Its rightly said Cadets are Half Soldiers too. In the span of three months, i got to develop strong habits and qualities which i will take forward throughout my life. This camp has surely taught me a lot and these memories will always secure a special place in my heart.

Cadet Prithvi Thakur

CVS NCC

Ncc company of College of Vocational Studies is a part of 2 Delhi Naval Unit, Okhla. Under the able command of Commander Santosh Kaimal(CO 2 DNU) and Lt. Commander Surender (ANO CVS), CVS NCC has experienced manifold progress.
From just 2 cadets in 2017, we have reached a strength of 50+ cadets, 15+ NSC holders, 4 RDC holders in just 3 years.
