All India Yatching Regatta Camp

Experiences

Sailing regatta is conducted every year at INS Chilka, Odisha. A total of 102 cadets (51 SDs + 51 SWs) participate in this competition over a period of 8 days, from all over India, representing all 17 NCC directorates. Sailing in itself is an art, it was really adventurous and a great experience. From doing practice to performing in competition it was a memorable time. During the practice, we learnt a lot of things, we made a lot of mistakes and eventually, we improved day by day and gave our best in the competition. From doing daily practices in river Yamuna and then, facing the competition at Chilka lake, Odisha, it was a lifetime experience.

Capturing the wind in the sail, using the right angle and the coordination between the helm and crew are important while sailing. While sailing, a person's mental strength plays a more prominent role than his/her physical strengths. While sailing, a person's mind should be calm and he/she should always be alert and well aware of their surroundings and ready to take quick actions!
We had a cultural night before the last day of the camp and it was amazing. All the directorates participated and showcased their cultures. We sang a song and the SDs did a group dance. The level of competition was very high specially in SD. SWs also did a great job, our boat got capsized and they recovered it really well. During the final race, the lake was on red alert. The waves were getting very high and the condition was not good, so the race was stopped in between and was continued on the next day. Delhi directorate stood 5th. On the final day, DG NCC distributed the medals and trophies to the winning directorate. In the end, we all sang the NCC song and a group photo was taken. On the next and final day, we had our picnic. We went to Puri Jagannath Temple and Sun Temple at Konark, it was amazing ending of the camp. The camp ended on this note with lots and lots of unforgettable memories.
