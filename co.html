2 Delhi Naval Unit

Situated in Okhala, 2 Delhi Naval Unit is one of the 2 naval units of Delhi Directorate and is being commanded by Commandar Santosh Kaimal

Commander Santosh Kaimal

Commander Santosh Kaima, who is the Commanding Officer of 2 Delhi Naval Unit NCC, is an Alumnus of Indian Naval Academy, Goa. Commisioned in 1998 as a Sub Lieutenant in Indian Navy in the Executive Branch. He is a Anti Submarine Warfare (ASW) Specialist and has served as Mine Counter Measure specialist in 21 MCM Squadron in the Eastern Fleet at Visakhapatnam and Anti Submarine Warfare Officer of front line warships like Indian Naval Ships Ranvir, Ranvijay and Talwar. The officer has commanded the Naval Training Ship Krishna and has been the Chief Instructor at Anti Submarine Warfare School, which trains Naval Personnel in Anti Submarine Warfare. Prior to taking over the command of 2DNU NCC, he was the Joint Director at Defence Staff Requirement at Ministry of Defence.
