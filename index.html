About Us

The National Cadet Corps is the youth wing of Armed Forces with its Headquarters at New Delhi, Delhi, India. It is open to school and college students on voluntary basis. National Cadet Corps is a Tri-Services Organisation, comprising the Army, Navy and Air Wing, engaged in grooming the youth of the country into disciplined and patriotic citizens.

Our Moto

Unity and Discipline NCC aims at discipline and unity among its cadets to instil the society with responsible, ambitious and self-controlled citizens and make our country a better one. Unity means the oneness, irrespective of the caste, color or creed.

Our Role

To groom the youth of the country into disciplined, responsible and patriotic citizens.NCC inculcates in the youth a sense of nationalism and secular outlook that contributes towards building a better nation.

Our Aim

The aim of the NCC is to develop character, discipline, leadership and the ideals of selfless service among the youth of the Nation. The Motto of NCC is “ Unity and discipline”. Besides giving thrill and excitement, NCC promotes camaraderie and resilience.
