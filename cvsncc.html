National Cadet Corps

The NCC company of CVS, unlike other NCC companies, is a heterogeneous institution with both SD and SW cadets. It is affiliated to 2 Delhi Naval Unit, Batla House, Okhla.NCC being the largest uniformed youth organisation of the world has provided many great leaders to the nation. NCC in CVS started with merely 2 cadets back in the year 2016. From that year the number of cadets enrolled has increased manifolds. Today the total number of cadets enrolled in CVS NCC is above 50. In the short span of 3 years we have accomplished a lot , a total of 14 cadets have been a part AINSC and 4 cadets have been a part of RDC, amongst them 2 are Rajpath holders.
Last year we successfully presented our first installment of Annual NCC Fest "VARUNAM". It was a huge success with around 700 cadets from all over the Delhi University becoming a part of it. CVS NCC provides a complete new arena for the cadets to develop the core of their skills and to establish themselves as completely new individuals. Development of physical as well as mental standards are the key criterias which are abided to develop the overall personality. CVS NCC is more of a family . Apart from training various friendly matches are played which tend to develop a value of camaraderie amongst the cadets. With all such activities it makes CVS NCC a way more than just NCC .

CVS NCC

Ncc company of College of Vocational Studies is a part of 2 Delhi Naval Unit, Okhla. Under the able command of Commander Santosh Kaimal(CO 2 DNU) and Lt. Commander Surender (ANO CVS), CVS NCC has experienced manifold progress.
From just 2 cadets in 2017, we have reached a strength of 50+ cadets, 15+ NSC holders, 4 RDC holders in just 3 years.
